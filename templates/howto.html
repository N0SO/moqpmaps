How To Enter you County Activation Plans

When the map initially loads, it will show the planned activations recorded in the database. See the Map Legend below for the color scheme. Mousing over a county will display the county name and a list of operator callsign(s) planning to activate that county.

Adding Your Activation Plans

To add your activation plans:

  1. Enter your callsign into the Callsign text box.
  2. Click on the New Plan button.
  3. Mouse over a county of interest. The county name, along with the callsign(s) of any operators planning to activate that county will be displayed.
  4. Click on a county to add it to your activation list. The selected county will turn red.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until your list is complete, then click the Save button. This will save your selections to the database under your callsign.
  6. Click the browser refresh button. Note that the newly selected counties will remain red, and will not appear when 'mousing over' a county on the map until this is completed.

Editing Your Activation Plans

To edit your activation plans:

  1. Enter your callsign into the Callsign text box.
  2. Click on the Load Plan button. Your previously selected counties will turn red. Clicking on a selected (red) counyt will remove it from your list. Clicking on non-red counties will add them to your list.
  3. Click the Save button and then refresh the browser when your edits are complete.
